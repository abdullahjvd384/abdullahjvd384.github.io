Our Approach: E-E-A-T Principles

We follow Google's E-E-A-T guidelines (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) in everything we publish:

  • Experience: Real-world testing and research of financial products and services
  • Expertise: In-depth analysis backed by industry data and expert sources
  • Authoritativeness: Citations from trusted institutions like the Insurance Information Institute, LIMRA, and Small Business Administration
  • Trustworthiness: Transparent methodology, no hidden affiliate relationships, regular content updates

Every article is researched using authoritative sources, updated regularly with current rates and regulations, and written to help you save money and make better decisions.
